The rules

To be the next owner of the Gold Bar, the current price is 0.05 bitcoins. After your purchase, the Gold Bars worth will increase by 15% (0.0075 bitcoins).

You will stay the owner until the next Gold Bar buyer purchases it for 0.0575 bitcoins. We then will send 0.055 bitcoins back to you. That means a 10% (0.005 btc) profit for you.

However, the Gold Bar resets randomly. The odds of it reseting are 3.125%, so your winning odds are 96.875%. Should your purchase trigger a reset, you'll lose your stake and the Gold Bars price goes back to 0.05 bitcoins. Please read FAQ for specific details.

Read the rules on the left and the FAQ below first before playing. Then, let us know your bitcoin address (this is where we'll send your profit). FAQ
What is this?

The bitcoin Gold Bar is an entertaining game, advertising opportunity, and a psychological experiment. You can think of it as a self-sustaining speculative bubble.

Why should I buy the Gold Bar?

Who wouldn't want the Gold Bar? Not only do you get free advertising and profit when the next buyer comes in, but you'll look cool doin it too.

How/when does the price increase?

Each time the Gold Bar changes owners its worth increases by 15%, rounded to 4 decimal places.

Why does the Gold Bar reset at all?

If it never reset, the price would go up forever. While this can be awesome to see, it would leave one gigantic loser at the end of the chain. Like any other speculative bubble, the Gold Bar has to collapse at some point.

When does the Gold Bar reset?

The Gold Bar resets in a completely random manner and can't be influenced in any way. Once your payment confirms, we take your transaction hash and the merkle root of the block containing your transaction and create another hash: hmac_sha512(merkleroot,txhash). If the decimal of the first two bytes of this hash are less than 2048 then you reset the Gold Bar. Since nobody can know the merkle root in advance there's no way to trick the system. This also lets you easily verify that resets were handled correctly.

So what are my odds of making a profit?

Technically speaking, your odds of making a 10% profit are 96.875%, assuming another buyer purchases the Gold Bar after you.

What happens if no one after me buys the Gold Bar?

If there's no purchase for 1 day, the Gold Bar will automatically reset to 0.05 bitcoins.

What happens if I send a payment but a few seconds before me another player buys the Gold Bar?

After a unique buying address is generated, purchases will be locked for 4 minutes so this should not happen. However, if an accidental double pay occurs, we will send a complete refund to the bitcoin address you provided and will include a public message via blockchain. Please note that refunds are made manually, so they could take a while.

I accidentally entered a wrong bitcoin address and my payout was sent to it can you refund me?

Payouts are automatic, once they're done the coins are on their way to the address you gave us and a refund is not possible. So please be sure to double check your address.

I don't trust you, how do I know you won't run away with my coins?

We try to make everything as transparent as possible, but you really should not play if you don't trust the bitcoin Gold Bar.

What about bugs and flaws?

Please tell us about bugs you find! We're using the blockchain.info API and can't guarantee 100% uptime. However, if there's a problem with their software or our server your transaction will still be in the block chain. We manually check incoming and outgoing transactions and will react accordingly.

I have another question, how can I contact you?

Just send an email to bitcoingoldbar@gmail.com. We'll be happy to help out.
